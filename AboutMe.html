About Me.

Hi! I'm Maxence, but everybody calls me Max. I'm an 18-year-old freshman from France studying graphic design in San Francisco. I'm from Lille in the north of France where I spent my whole childhood. I always got interest in other cultures and countries around the world! I especially like the North American culture and Asian culture.

I'm passionate about Art. Especially GraphicDesign as a whole. I also like Geography, History, Learning Languages, Asian Art History and Cats.
